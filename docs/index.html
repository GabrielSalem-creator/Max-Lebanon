What MAX is, and how it keeps you safe

The full picture — capabilities, languages, speed and privacy.

🎯 The mission

Protect Lebanese citizens through hands-free automation: cut distracted-driving accidents by letting people talk to MAX instead of looking at a screen, surface real-time security alerts, and handle everyday digital tasks so people save time and stay safe.

🧩 Capabilities

  • Voice, three languages — English, French and Lebanese Arabic, for both listening and speaking.
  • Road & security — live traffic, safest routes, and real-time incident/security awareness across Lebanon.
  • Local life — daily fuel prices, Lebanon news, restaurants with hours & numbers, and Summer 2026 events.
  • Productivity — WhatsApp by voice, calendar read & add, PowerPoints, AI images and video.
  • Fast-path skills — common tasks are predefined, so they run instantly instead of being reasoned from scratch.

⚡ Speed by design

MAX uses trajectory memory: the first time it does a task it works out the steps; every time after, it recognises the request, replays the proven recipe and only changes the variables — dropping minutes to seconds. It also tells you up front whether a task is new ("first time, give me a moment") or known ("done this before, replaying now").

🔒 Privacy & security

  • Password-protected app — max.vdo-x.art is locked behind a login with a saved session, so no spam or abuse.
  • Human-in-the-loop logins — when a site needs a password, you type it into a secure local prompt; MAX injects it straight into the page and never reads or stores it.
  • Local-first — sensitive data and credentials stay on your machine, never in the AI's context.
